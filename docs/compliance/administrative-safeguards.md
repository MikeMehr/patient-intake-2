# Administrative Safeguards

## Governance and Accountability

- Designate:
  - HIPAA Security Officer
  - HIPAA Privacy Officer
- Define control owners for each launch safeguard.

## Policies and Procedures

- Maintain and review:
  - HIPAA Security Policy
  - HIPAA Privacy Policy
  - Incident response policy
  - Access management policy
  - Sanctions policy

## Workforce Management

- Require HIPAA and security awareness training before production access.
- Run annual refresher training and track attestations.
- Apply sanctions policy consistently for policy violations.

## Vendor and Third-Party Oversight

- Maintain a BAA register for all PHI-touching vendors.
- Track BAA execution status, effective dates, and renewals.
- Validate vendor HIPAA posture before enabling PHI workflows.

## Risk Management

- Maintain active risk register and acceptance records.
- Require expiration dates for temporary risk acceptance.
- Tie every accepted risk to compensating controls and closure criteria.

## Documentation Retention

- Keep compliance evidence and approvals versioned and reviewable.
- Preserve audit/compliance records per policy and legal requirements.
